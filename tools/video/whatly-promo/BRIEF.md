## Intent
A punchy ~30s promo for **Whatly**, a feature-rich desktop client for WhatsApp
Web (MIT-licensed fork of WhatSie). Modern, energetic, brand-forward — teal
identity (#0d9488 → #064e46, accent #2dd4bf). Music-driven montage, no narration.
For the snapcraft.io store listing (uploaded to YouTube). Show-it-as-is: the
existing promo "feature cards" are the hero visuals, animated with real motion.

## Assets
Feature cards + brand, all 1280x720 PNG unless noted, in the repo at docs/img/:
- card-main-dark.png — the whole app, dark theme (hero)
- card-main-light.png — the whole app, light theme (hero)
- card-scheduled.png — scheduled messages (flagship new feature)
- card-chat.png — chat themes + privacy blur
- card-themes.png — fourteen chat themes
- card-accounts.png — multiple accounts
- card-lock.png — app lock
- card-spellcheck.png — multi-language spell checker
- card-shortcuts.png — keyboard shortcuts
- card-tray.png — smarter system tray
- card-notifications.png — native notifications
- card-installers.png — snap/Flatpak/AppImage/deb/RPM/AUR/Windows (outro)
- net.shakaran.whatly.png (256²) — the teal app logo, at
  dist/linux/hicolor/256x256/apps/net.shakaran.whatly.png

## Customizations
- No-capture: use the supplied cards, do not scrape a site.
- Music bed (local engine), upbeat/modern, no voice-over.
- Real motion: animated logo intro, cards entering with 3D/keyframed motion,
  kinetic titles, teal-branded transitions; "available everywhere" outro.
